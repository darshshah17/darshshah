Introduction to Coding

You may be wondering how I got introduced to the world of coding. Well, in June 2020 (summer of grade 9), I began exploring a website called scratch. Here, I was able to gain my first informal introduction to coding. I began writing and animating the code for an eagle chasing a rabbit. I drew each frame with precision and used each block available to my advantage. Eventually, I finished and saw my first code in action. I was proud, but it was here that I saw the true disadvantages of block-based coding. I knew I had to move forward. That same summer, I learnt the basics of python and applied my knowledge to create a guessing game. The objective was simple, try and guess a word from a premade, imported list. You'll get hints based on the difficulty you choose to play! After this, I continued to use my coding knowledge to build sophisticated projects that I have linked under: Projects
